About The Garhwal Rifles

The Garhwal Rifles is one of the prestigious infantry regiments of the Indian Army. It was established in 1887 and consists mainly of soldiers from the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand. The regiment is known for its bravery, discipline, and loyalty.

History

The regiment was raised in 1887 as the 39th (The Garhwal) Regiment of Bengal Infantry. It served with distinction in World War I, World War II, and various operations after India's independence including the Kargil War.

Achievements

  • Participation in World War I
  • Participation in World War II
  • Gallantry Awards including Param Vir Chakra
  • Bravery in Kargil War (1999)
  • Service in UN Peacekeeping Missions
